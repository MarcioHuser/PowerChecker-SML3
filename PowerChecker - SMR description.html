Check if your fuses can blow!



With this mod you can place down a monitor to verify if your current power production can run your factory at 100% without tripping the fuse. It is Multiplayer compatible. Many thanks to TwoTwoEleven (Micro Manage) for the orientations on how to get it working üòÄ

What it includes:

A building with a screen that shows:
  • How much power can be generated at the circuit;
  • How much power is currently being consumed;
  • The maximum amount of power the circuit will consume if everything runs at the same time;
  • How much power you have to spare at the circuit.
  • When interacted it will show a popup with the same stats, plus a list of all the generators and consumers of the circuit, grouped by:
    • Type
    • Power generated/consumed
    • Overclock
    It will also show the sum of consumed/generated power of each building group


How dows it work:

The screen and the power light will change color according to the given statuses:
  • If the maximum amount is greater than the maximum capacity, the panel will show red and the power light will blink with red color. It will also show red if no power is available;
  • If the spare power is lower than a given limit, the panel and the power light will show yellow;
  • Otherwise, the screen will be black and the power light will be green
